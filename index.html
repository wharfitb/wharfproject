TRIUMPH

The Tsunamigenic Landslide and Structural Modelling of the Nieuwerkerk Seamount: Potential and Hypothesis (TRIUMPH) Project aims to describe the morphology of the seamount to further conduct landslide-induced tsunami modelling in the vicinity of the Ina Lao (formerly Nieuwerkerk) seamount.

HYDROGRAPHY ITB

Hydrography Research Group is part of the Faculty of Earth Sciences and Technology, Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB) with the missions of:

  • 1 Fostering action and multidisciplinary research,
  • 2 Engaging with the coastal and small islands community,
  • 3 Contribution to standard practices in the industry, and
  • 4 Advancing world-class education.

Further information are available through our website hidrografi.gd.itb.ac.id
